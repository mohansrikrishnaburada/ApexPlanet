Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Combine hot pasta with pancetta, then mix in egg mixture off heat.
  5. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

Homemade Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • 100g tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F).
  2. Roll out dough and spread tomato sauce.
  3. Add mozzarella and basil leaves.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve hot.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 100g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 100g chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then add egg and vanilla.
  3. Mix in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.

Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons (juice and zest)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with lemon juice, zest, oil, garlic, rosemary, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes per side.
  4. Check internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Serve with lemon wedges.
